Radhe Shyam

Description

Radhe Shyam is a 2022 Indian Telugu-language period romantic drama film written and directed by Radha Krishna Kumar. Produced by UV Creations and T-Series, the film was shot simultaneously in Telugu and Hindi languages and stars Prabhas and Pooja Hegde with an ensemble supporting cast. Set in 1970s Italy, the film revolves around a young astrologer named Vikramaditya, who is conflicted between his destiny and his love for Dr. Prerana.
